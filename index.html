Movemeber Shave-Off

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur.

Last November, The Prefects held their annual Movember campaign, Spread the Stache. Over 50 students participated in the campaign by collecting pledges while several others, both students and teachers, took the initiative to grow a month-long mustache or beard for the final Shave-Off. The Prefects have done an outstanding job of fundraising for Movember Canada. Keep up the great work!
